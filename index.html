About Me

I am a dedicated and results-oriented Data Analyst with over 3 years of experience in analyzing, interpreting, and visualizing data to drive informed decision-making. My expertise lies in leveraging tools like R, Python, SQL, and Excel to work with raw datasets, transform them into actionable insights, and support strategic business outcomes. My proficiency in R programming and RStudio allows me to excel in data visualization and advanced statistical analysis. I have worked extensively on data preparation and visualization projects, showcasing my ability to turn complex datasets into meaningful visuals. I also have hands-on experience with the ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) process using SQL, ensuring efficient data integration and management. In addition to my technical expertise, I have a strong foundation in Excel, enabling me to perform detailed analyses and create dynamic dashboards. Driven by curiosity and a passion for solving problems, I thrive on uncovering insights that lead to impactful decision-making. Whether working with raw data or building polished data pipelines, I bring a meticulous and innovative approach to every project.
